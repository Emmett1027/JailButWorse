Gameplay

Prison, PvP, and pure chaos.

How to Play

JailButWorse offers a unique take on the prison experience — chaos, progression, and community-driven mayhem. Here’s what to expect:

  • Start Small: Become a prisoner and get sent to jail but worse, then expand into being a guard, criminal, etc.
  • Rank Up: Escape and click the get gear signs to become a CRIMINAL, or become warden!
  • Fight Back: Heavy on PVP, fight other players to the death (ingame)..
  • Trade & Economy: Buy, sell, and trade with others for rare gear and tools.
  • Events: Participate in daily events hosted by staff to earn exclusive rewards.
  • Endgame: Only the strongest can escape the prison alive — or rule it from within.
