Gameplay Rules

Rules for gameplay.

  1. No unfair mods, hacks, macros, or cheats.
    • No mods that allow seeing through walls.
  2. No random killing, spawn killing, or random cuffing.
  3. Bugs are allowed ONLY if reported and given permission to use. Silent abuse results in punishment.
  4. Don’t bully or intentionally ruin others’ gameplay. (Such as spam soli then farm)
  5. Alt accounts are allowed, but not for warden sniping or kill farming.

Summary

Play fair. No cheats, no random killing, no abusing bugs or alt accounts.

Extra Info

Spawnkilling

Killing a prisoner while they are still in their cell and haven’t moved.

Randomkilling

Killing a prisoner for no valid reason.

You MAY kill a prisoner if:

  • They provoked you or killed someone
  • They are outside the prison
  • They have contraband
  • They are in the Black Market
  • it is Lockdown, Lights Out, or Cell Time and they are outside their cell
  • It is Roll Call and they are not on or going to the red sand
  • They are in the Warden’s Office without permission
  • It is KOTC and they are inside the ring

Summary

You cannot kill prisoners for no reason. If they provoke you or break prison rules, killing is allowed.

Chat Rules

  1. Slurs or extreme language result in an instant ban.
  2. Limit swearing.
  3. No filter bypassing.
  4. No toxicity.
  5. No spam or chat flooding.
  6. No real-life threats or wishing harm.
  7. No mute evasion.
  8. English only.
  9. Do not impersonate staff.
  10. Don't advertise stuff unless given permission to.

Summary

Don’t be toxic, don’t spam, don’t bypass filters, don't advertise, and don’t impersonate staff.

Other Rules

  1. Do not abuse rules to target others.
  2. No racism or sexism.
  3. Be appropriate.
  4. No punishment evading.
  5. Use common sense.

Summary

Don’t be weird, don’t evade punishments, and don’t twist rules.
