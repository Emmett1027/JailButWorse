About Us

The story behind JailButWorse.

Our Mission

Well, JailButWorse was just an idea in the heads of the founders, when the other PrisonButBad-inspired server, NeoPBB, just died. It literally had no development, so there's no suprise. All of the bugs I knew personally weren't patched, and it was just growing deader by the second. So, my friend Dejslab and I decided to do something about it. Make our own PrisonButBad inspired server! And no, this isn't what you'd call a 'copy', it's even made in a different coding language, with many different features!

Meet the Team

  • Admins: Make/oversee gameplay, rules, build, and server updates.
  • Moderators: Maintain chat moderation and help players with issues.
  • Builders & Developers: Design custom maps, systems, and experiences that make JailButWorse stand out.

We’re always trying to do funny stuff. If you have a suggestion, say it!
