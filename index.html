Server Rules

At JailButWorse, we maintain a strict but fair environment for all players. Our rules are designed to ensure a positive experience for everyone:

  • No griefing or destruction of other players' builds
  • Respect all staff members and other players
  • No cheating, hacking, or exploiting server mechanics
  • Follow the in-game chat rules and community guidelines
  • Do not use any unauthorized third-party software
  • Report any rule violations to staff immediately

Breaking these rules will result in appropriate punishment, which may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity.

How to Play

Getting started on JailButWorse is simple and fun:

  1. Connect to our server at jailbutworse.com
  2. Choose your starting position in the prison area
  3. Explore the jail environment and interact with other players
  4. Complete missions to earn rewards and progress
  5. Build and decorate your personal cell or escape routes
  6. Participate in community events and challenges

For more detailed instructions, check out our comprehensive guide in the game's help menu.

About Us

JailButWorse was created by a group of passionate Minecraft enthusiasts who wanted to bring a unique twist to the traditional prison survival experience.

Our team has been developing and maintaining this server for over 3 years, constantly adding new features and improving gameplay mechanics. We focus on creating an engaging environment where players can enjoy both solo and collaborative activities.

We value community input and regularly update our server based on player feedback. Our staff is dedicated to maintaining a fair and enjoyable environment for everyone.
